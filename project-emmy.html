This Emmy Award-winning composite was recognized by The National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences. The piece combined original animation, motion graphics, and special effects into a cohesive visual work that earned recognition from industry professionals at the national level.

The composite brought together multiple visual layers — original motion graphics, animated elements, and post-production effects — into a single cohesive piece. Every element was built from scratch, designed to work together with intention and precision.
